Stir Fried Chciken

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless,skinless chicken breast
  • Olive oil 2 Tablespoons
  • Broccoli Florets 2 Cups
  • 1/2 yellow bell pepper cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1/2 red bell pepper cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1/2 cup baby carrots sliced

Preperation of the Spicy Mayo

  1. Mix the mayo and thin it out a bit
  2. Add the Ingredients thats mentioned above in the respected mesaure ammount
  3. Taste the sauce
  4. *If the sauce needs a bit more spicy, feel free to add more Sriracha
  5. Enjoy
