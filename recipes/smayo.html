Spicy Mayo

Ingredients

  • Mayo 1/4 Cup
  • Sriracha Hot Sauce 2 Tablespoons
  • Fresh lime juice 1 Teaspoon
  • Toasted seasme oil 1/2 Teaspoon

Preperation of the Spicy Mayo

  1. Mix the mayo and thin it out a bit
  2. Add the Ingredients thats mentioned above in the respected mesaure ammount
  3. Taste the sauce
  4. *If the sauce needs a bit more spicy, feel free to add more Sriracha
  5. Enjoy
